Refund Policy

Thank you for choosing Through Equity.
We understand that sometimes circumstances may require you to request a refund for a course or service purchased from us. Please read this policy carefully to understand your rights and obligations when seeking a refund.

Refund Eligibility:
  • Refunds are only available for courses or services purchased directly from our website.
  • To be eligible for a refund, you must submit a refund request within 7 days of the enrollment date.
  • Refunds will not be provided for courses or services that have been partially or fully completed.
  • We reserve the right to deny refund requests if we believe they are made in bad faith or are an abuse of our refund policy.
  • Verbal disrespect towards Through Equity team members or any activities deemed inappropriate by the Through Equity team (e.g., unsolicited unprofessional messages) may result in the denial of refund requests.
    • How to Request a Refund:

    To request a refund, please contact our customer support team at [email address] with the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Date of enrolment
  • Reason for the refund request
    • Refund Process:

    Once we receive your refund request, we will review it and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

  • If your refund is approved, it will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 3 days.
  • If your refund request is denied, we will provide you with an explanation for the decision.
    • Exceptions:

    Please note that certain circumstances may prevent us from offering refunds, including but not limited to:

  • Refunds requested outside of the specified refund period.
  • Refunds requested for courses or services that have already been accessed or completed.
  • Refunds requested for courses or services purchased by third-party platforms or partners.
