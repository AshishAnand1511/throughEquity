Trading

Dive into the dynamic world of trading with our comprehensive courses designed to equip you with essential skills and knowledge. From technical analysis to option trading, our courses empower you to make informed decisions and thrive in the financial markets.

- Description:

Dive into the world of technical analysis and learn how to analyse price charts and indicators to make informed trading decisions. This course is designed for beginners and intermediate traders looking to enhance their technical analysis skills.

- Key Topics:
  • Reading Charts e.g., support and resistance, trendlines
  • Understanding chart patterns (e.g., Head & shoulder, wedges, Rounding bottom etc)
  • Utilising technical indicators (e.g., moving averages, RSI etc)
  • Recognizing market trends and patterns for entry and exit points
  • Developing trading strategies based on technical analysis
    • - Skills & Outcomes:
  • Ability to analyse price charts effectively
  • Proficiency in using technical indicators to identify trading opportunities
  • Improved decision-making skills in timing trades
  • Development of personalised trading strategies based on technical analysis principles
    • - Description:

    Explore the world of options analysis and trading and discover advanced strategies to leverage market movements. Whether you're new to options or looking to enhance your trading skills, this course covers essential concepts and practical techniques to succeed in the options market.

    - Key Topics:
  • Introduction to Derivatives (Futures & options) terminology
  • Option Chain Analysis
  • Option Trading Using Technical Analysis
  • Understanding option pricing and Greeks (delta, gamma, theta, vega)
  • Basic and advanced option strategies (e.g., covered calls, spreads, straddles)
  • Risk management techniques in options trading
    • - Skills & Outcomes:
  • Ability to confidently trade options contracts
  • Understanding of various option strategies and when to apply them
  • Proficiency in managing risk and maximising returns in options trading
  • Practical application of options trading strategies in real-world scenarios
