Research Analyst

The NISM Research Analyst course teaches you all about working as a financial research analyst. You'll dive into understanding how the stock market operates, learn to analyse economic trends, and master the art of evaluating companies and industries to make informed investment choices.

- Key Topics:
  • introduction to research analyst profession
  • introduction to securities marke
  • terminology in equity and debt market
  • Fundamentals Of Research
  • Economic Analysis
  • Industry Analysis
  • Company Analysis : Business And Governance
  • Company analysis: Financial Analysis
  • Corporate Actions
  • Valuation Principals
  • Fundamental Of Risk And Return
  • Qualities Of Good Research Report
  • Legal And regulatory Environment
    • - Benefits and Skills Acquired::
  • Gain a clear understanding of the role of a research analyst in finance.
  • Deep understanding of the roles and responsibilities of a research analyst within the financial industry.
  • Ability to conduct thorough research, analyse financial statements, and evaluate investment opportunities.
  • Learn to analyse big-picture economic trends and their impact on investments.
  • Ethical considerations in research and adherence to legal and regulatory requirements, ensuring professionalism and integrity in financial analysis.
